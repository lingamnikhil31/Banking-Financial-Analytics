# 🏦 Banking & Financial Analytics

> Transforming customer banking data into actionable financial insights using SQL and Power BI.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

**Banking & Financial Analytics** is an end-to-end data analytics project focused on understanding customer financial behavior and analyzing banking portfolio performance.

The project analyzes customer demographics, income, loans, deposits, savings, credit cards, business lending, properties, risk weighting, and customer loyalty to generate meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze banking data and answer key business questions such as:

- How large is the bank's customer base?
- What is the total value of deposits and loans?
- Which customer segments have the highest financial value?
- How are customers distributed across income bands and loyalty classifications?
- What is the relationship between bank loans and deposits?
- How much do customers contribute through savings and checking accounts?
- What are the major financial trends across the customer portfolio?

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| 👥 Total Customers | Total number of unique banking customers |
| 💰 Total Bank Deposits | Total value of customer deposits |
| 🏦 Total Loan Portfolio | Total value of bank loans |
| 📈 Average Estimated Income | Average estimated customer income |
| ⚖️ Loan-to-Deposit Ratio | Measures lending relative to deposits |
| 💳 Average Credit Card Balance | Average customer credit card balance |
| 🏢 Total Business Lending | Total value of business lending |
| 💵 Total Savings | Total value held in savings accounts |
| 🏧 Total Checking Balance | Total value held in checking accounts |
| ⭐ Customer Loyalty Distribution | Analysis of customer loyalty classifications |
